## What are cookies?

Cookies are text files stored on your device by a website's server. Each cookie contains a unique identifier, website's domain name, and other data to associate you. Cookies and other related measures are commonly used to track internet users across websites and devices, usually for marketing purposes.

## Cookies by fortrabbit

We limit the usage of cookies for our web properties (www, docs, blog and dashboard) to functional cookies. They help to offer a convenient experience and let us recognize that you are logged in, or have set a certain color scheme. These cookies are created and used only by us and not shared with any third party. This applies to all cookies related to the `fortrabbit.com` domain.

## Cookies by fortrabbit sub-contractors

Certain functionalities of our services are not provided by us directly, but integrated via a third party. This includes but is not limited to:

- Git provider
- Payment processing
- Support chat
- Status page

These services are storing their own cookies and have their own respective policies. We aim to make the usage of these third-party integrations optional where possible and make it visible wherever a third-party service is used.

## How to delete cookies?

You can restrict or block cookies through your browser settings.
